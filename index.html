The Big Day!

  • 4:15 PM ✧ Welcome to East Lynn

    Our wedding venue is a lovely farm & BnB in Round Hill, VA - about a 40 minute drive from Chantilly.

    Come to the patio behind the brick house to enjoy some beautiful views of East Lynn's gardens before the ceremony.


    You can follow these Directions from the Dulles Courtyard Marriott.

    Parking is limited so please carpool if possible! We've set up a Carpool Signup to help organize rides.

    When you arrive, please park by the barn on the left side , not the brick house on the right.

  • 5:00 PM ✧ Wedding Ceremony

    The ceremony will take place in the field behind the brick house , facing the trees & mountains.

    It will be officiated by Pastor Tom Berlin.

  • 5:30 PM ✧ Cocktails

    After the ceremony, head across the street to the barn for cocktail hour!

    We'll be serving our signature cocktail, wine, beer, soft drinks, and yummy appetizers.

  • 7:00 PM ✧ Dinner

    Make your way to your assigned table upstairs in the hayloft.

    After our entrance, it's dinner time!

    Enjoy some freshly made wood-fired pizza and delicious salads from Timber Pizza Co.

  • 8:00 PM ✧ Reception & Dance Party

    After dinner, it's time to dance to some spicy beats!

  • 10:50 PM ✧ Send Off

    We're gonna do the thing where we drive off in a cool car, you gotta be there.

FAQ

  • Whatever shall I wear??

    Something rather dashing that you can comfortably walk around a field or dance in!

    Soooo, maybe leave the stiletto heels at home.

    ✧ Pro tip ✧ Bring sunglasses for the ceremony and a light sweater or jacket for the evening.

  • Do I bring a gift??

    If you would like to get us a gift, please check out our registry!

    We only ask that you please do not bring boxed gifts to the wedding. Instead, please ship to the Seattle address provided on the registry.

    ✧ Pro tip ✧ We'd love contributions to our honeymoon fund - just scroll to the bottom of the registry!

  • How do I get there??

    You can follow these Directions from the Dulles Courtyard Marriott.

    ✧ Pro tip ✧ Going by route 50 will take you through some winding country roads, which makes for a nice trip on the way in, but you may want to take the toll road 267 on the way back when it's dark.

  • Can I get a ride??

    Totally! Check out our carpool signup!

    ✧ Pro tip ✧ On the way, listen to the latest episode of our favorite podcast, My Brother, My Brother, and Me.
