The Big Day!

  • 4:30 PM ･ Welcome to East Lynn

    Our wedding venue is a lovely farm & BnB in Round Hill, VA - about a 40 minute drive from Chantilly.

    The ceremony starts at 5, so please arrive a bit early so you have time to get settled!

    You can follow these Directions from the Dulles Courtyard Marriott.

    When you arrive, please park by the barn on the left side of the road, not the brick house on the right. Parking is very limited so please carpool if possible! We've set up a Carpool Signup to help organize rides.

  • 5:00 PM ･ Wedding Ceremony

    The ceremony will take place in the field behind the brick house, facing the trees.

    It will be officiated by Pastor Tom Berlin.

  • 5:30 PM ･ Cocktails

    After the ceremony, head over to the patio behind the brick house!

    We'll be serving wine, beer, and some non-alcoholic beverages, along with some light appetizers.

  • 7:00 PM ･ Dinner

    Around 6:30, head over to the barn loft.

    After our entrance and first dance, it's dinner time!

    Enjoy some freshly made wood-fired pizza and other delicious food from Timber Pizza Co!

  • 8:00 PM ･ Reception & Dance Party

    After dinner it's time to party!

    Be ready to dance to some spicy beats!

  • 10:50 PM ･ Send Off

    We're gonna do the thing where we drive off in a cool car, you gotta be there.
